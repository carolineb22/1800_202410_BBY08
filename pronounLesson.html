Pronouns


Some Vocabulary

  • chen = I
  • chexw = you (singular)
  • ents = I/me (emphatic)
  • chet = we
  • i7x̱w swat = everyone
  • kwétsiw̓it = they (not close by)
  • ta néwyap = you all (used when addressing a group)
  • ta new = you (used when calling out to someone)

Words in Action

Today was so fun! Chen went out to the park with my friends, chet had a picnic and got to share delicious snacks! I7x̱w swat had such a fun time! Chet played games and talked for hours. Finally Alex and Amanda said they had to leave so we gave them some leftover cupcakes and said goodbye. Kwétsiw̓it texted us half an hour later saying Alex forgot his jackets so chen drove by their house to give it back.
