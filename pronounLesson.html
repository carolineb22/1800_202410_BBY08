Nature

  • chen = I
  • chexw = you (singular)
  • chet = we
  • ents = I/me (emphatic)
  • i7x̱w swat = everyone
  • kwétsiw̓it = they (not close by)
  • ta néwyap = you all (used in addressing a group)
  • ta new = you (used in calling out to someone)
