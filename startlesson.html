The Start!


Some Vocabulary

  • I chen tl'iḵ = I have arrived (used as a greeting)
  • I chexw tl'iḵ = You have arrived (response to the above greeting)
  • Nuuw = Hello
  • Nú = Hello (to close friends)
  • Huy chexw a = Thank you
  • Huy̓ melh halh = Goodbye

Words in Action

Today is Aiyana's birthday I am going to her house to celebrate! First I get on the bus a say "Nuuw" to the bus driver. He smiles at me and I go sit down. When I get off the bus I call out behind me "Huy chexw a". I walk to her house and push open the back gate, "I chen tl'iḵ!" I call out to my bestfriend. She replies "Nú" and gives me a hug. "Happy birthday!" I say and hand her my gift. After many hours of fun it's finally time to leave and we say "Huy̓ melh halh".



Check Your Understanding (answer in Squamish!)
