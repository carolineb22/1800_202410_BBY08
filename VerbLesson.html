Verbs


Some Vocabulary

  • p’i7t = grab, take, seize
  • ḵ’ayt = call out, shout, holler
  • éx̱wu7n = cough
  • ch’iin = lift something
  • kwílten = fight/wrestle
  • héwa7s = bring something along
  • kw’ay̓ = be/get hungry
  • ínexw = view something
  • huyá7 = go away, leave
  • kw’enmán = commend, congratulate, thank
  • is = keep something here
  • kway = hide
  • ch’em̓ = bite
  • kat = climb, go up

Words in Action

Today is the sports day at my school! There are all sorts of events, and even though I don't usually participate, it's fun to watch. First I watch the soccer game, it's the biggest event and there are people who kat the sides of the bleachers to watch. The players kwílten to win the ball and I hear the crowd ḵ’ayt as the blue teams shoots at the net. I start to feel very kw’ay̓ so I huyá7 to get a hotdog from the stand selling them. I take a huge ch’em̓ and immediately regret it, I forgot how much I hate hotdogs....
