More Information

Our goal at pathways is more than just a language learning app. We want this to be an information hub and a place to connect with community. We have compiled a list of resources for a wide variety of indigenous groups in Canada.

Squamish Resources

Squamish Nation

Sḵwx̱wú7mesh Úxwumixw (Squamish Nation) is a hub for Squamish people and those wanting to learn more about their culture the website has information about oppurtunities in the community, events and gatherings, services for squamish people and more.


Squamish Nation Talking Dictionary

This is a section of the Squamish Nation website that is dedicated to their language. While they have a limited vocabulary the information is easy to access for anyone.


General Resources

Metis Nation BC

Metis Nation BC is a resource for metis people living in British Columbia, you can apply for citizenship and access services and access indigenous services and information about the Metis culture.


Canadian Government Learning Resources

Canadian Government Learning Resources is a hub with a lot of internal and external links to learn about the three indigenous groups in Canada as well as things that effect all three.
