Family


Some Vocabulary

  • elhtách = parents
  • chésha7 = mother
  • man = father
  • men̓ = child
  • mén̓men = children
  • áyish = sibling of opposite gender
  • kwúpits = older sibling/cousin
  • kwepkwúpits = older siblings
  • si7l = grandparent
  • ímats = grandchild
  • kwtams = husband
  • chewásh = wife

Words in Action

This year we're having my man's birthday dinner at my kwúpits's house. This would be fine except a few months back I noticed that my kwúpits had blocked me on all social mendia platforms and blocked my phone number without saying anything to me. I love my man and don't want to miss his special day but I don't know what's happening with my áyish. I decided to call my chésha7 to ask if she knew what had happened but she told me she doesn't know but that I should come anyways. I hope my sister's chewásh isn't mad at me as well or this will be an awkward dinner!
