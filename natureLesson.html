Some Vocabulary

  • chilh temíxw = heaven
  • in̓inyáx̱a7n = thunder, Thunderbird, airplane
  • ḵ’eḵsín̓ ti siyátshen = world, universe
  • spáḵ’em = flower
  • slhem̓xw = rain
  • spahím̓ = wind
  • sch’úlha7 = leaf
  • kwúsen = star
  • skwayl = sky

Words in Action

Sometimes when I need a break I take a walk to my local park. Today was one of those days, so I put on my coat and braced myself for the slhem̓xw and spahím̓. The walk to the park is always nice, I get to say hi to my neighbor's dogs that have seemingly dragged their owners outside on this miserable night. But this is where I feel at peace. I look up at the skwayl to admire the bright kwúsen I see every night. As I finally get to the park I see the most wonderful thing. A spáḵ’em has broken through the first spring frost and is poking out to admire the ḵ’eḵsín̓ ti siyátshen with me.
