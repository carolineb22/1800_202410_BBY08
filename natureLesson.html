Nature

  • chilh temíxw = heaven
  • in̓inyáx̱a7n = thunder, Thunderbird, airplane
  • ḵ’eḵsín̓ ti siyátshen = world, universe
  • spáḵ’em = flower
  • slhem̓xw = rain
  • spahím̓ = wind
  • sch’úlha7 = leaf
  • kwúsen = star
  • skwayl = sky

Words in Action

The Native American worldview of the ḵ’eḵsín̓ ti siyátshenis influenced heavily by the observing of the slhem̓xw and the spáḵ’em. A raw connection to nature it is when we feel the spahím̓ brushing our face as we look up at the chilh temíxw, filled with night and kwúsen. Almost nothing is more revealing than that.
