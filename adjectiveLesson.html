Adjectives


Some Vocabulary

  • eḵwís = narrow
  • ch’ey̓xw = dry
  • etsím = small
  • hiyí = big
  • kw'as = hot, warm
  • smáts’en = arrogant, proud (arrogant pride)
  • esḵénaxw = greedy
  • nach’ím̓ = beautiful/handsome

Words in Action

Today and I went to the shelter to find a cat to adopt. An attendant showed me the available cats and I was amazed at what I saw. There was a hiyí fluffy cat who was gobboling down his breakfast, so esḵénaxw! In the cage right beside it was a very etsím kitten. I was afraid talking to loud would scare it away as it was so tiny and frail. But it walked straight up to the bars of it's cage and stuck it's head straight through the eḵwís bars to meowed at me! The third cat was a senior, 14 years old and he had been at the shelter for a year already! When I approached his cage he stood up, stretched and came to the bars, showing off how nach’ím̓ he was.
