Food


Some Vocabulary

  • seplín = bread
  • sḵ’ey̓ = smoked fish
  • slhum̓ = soup
  • yetwán = red salmonberry
  • sḵw’elám = berry
  • smeyts = meat

Words in Action

Tonight I'm in charge of making dinner for the family! I decided to make the best slhum̓ my family has ever had! I started early, baking some seplín and made a spread with sḵw’elám I picked yesterday. The soup had a vegtable base. I used the carrots and potates we grew just outside, and too fill us up I made sure there was lots of smeyts. The slhum̓ was ready so I went outside to call everyone in for dinner. Just then I caught a whiff of the neighbors dinner. Awh- now I wish I had sḵ’ey̓!
