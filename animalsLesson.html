Animals


Some Vocabulary

  • push = cat
  • hew̓ít = rat
  • kweshú = pig
  • kw’át’an = mouse
  • élhḵay̓ = snake
  • ḵw’iyítḵ = seagull
  • ḵ’iyí7ch = elk, moose
  • ch’ésḵen = golden eagle
  • kshaws = blue jay, Stellar's jay
  • kwu7s = spring salmon
  • chíyatmixw = great horned owl
  • ḵwen̓ímach = mosquito

Words in Action

I grew up living with my push Charlie. To me Charlie was the coolest thing ever. But when my parents started to take me out on hikes with them as I got older I learned about all the other animals in our world! If you watched the river closely sometimes you can see the kwu7s jumping through the rapids, or a élhḵay̓ slithering their way through the tall grasses. They come in so many different colours it's amazing! At night I can hear the hoot of the chíyatmixw that lives in the forest nearby. My dad says that he's looking for dinner, and his favourite thing to find is a little kw’át’an! Everyday I learn more and more about the wonderful animals we share this earth with, but if you ask me, Charlie's still the coolest!
